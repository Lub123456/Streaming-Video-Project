==============================
Video Folder Requirements
==============================

Place all your video files in this folder.

Each file must follow the naming format:
- <name>-<resolution>.<format>

Examples:
- myfilm-240p.mp4
- MovieName-720p.avi
- Test_Movie-1080p.mkv

Accepted video formats:
- mp4
- avi
- mkv

Only files with these formats and naming convention will be detected and processed.
All other files will be ignored automatically.
